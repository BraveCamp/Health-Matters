What is the Real Purpose of Gatorade

If you're like me, I drink Gatorade like an everday drink. Gatorade comes in 3 series:pregame fuel,thirst quencher and post-game protein recovery(as seen above). Gatorade is really for building back up your electrolytes after a tough work or while playing a sport. If you're just drinking gatorade on a daily basis without actually being active,you're really just drinking sugar water with calories.
